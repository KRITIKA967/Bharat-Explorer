Ladakh

The land of high passes and stark deserts, Ladakh is a stunning fusion of rugged terrain, Buddhist culture, and otherworldly beauty. From the azure Pangong Lake and ancient monasteries to windswept valleys and starry skies, it embodies the raw spirit of the Himalayas with silence and strength.

It is home to dramatic mountainscapes, crystal-clear skies, and an enduring spiritual legacy rooted in Tibetan Buddhism.
