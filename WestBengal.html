West Bengal

West Bengal , A culturally rich state in eastern India, known for its vibrant arts, literature, and history.

It stretches from the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas in the north to the tranquil beaches of the Bay of Bengal in the south.
Home to the bustling metropolis of Kolkata, the lush tea gardens of Darjeeling, and the mystical mangroves of the Sundarbans. The state’s diverse landscapes, heritage, and warm hospitality make it a unique travel destination, where age-old traditions blend seamlessly with modern influences.
From the echoes of Rabindranath Tagore’s verses to the flavors of street-side puchkas, every corner of West Bengal tells a story of resilience and celebration
