Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu , located in the southernmost part of India, is a state known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning architecture, and diverse landscapes.

It is bordered by Kerala to the west, Karnataka to the northwest, Andhra Pradesh to the north, and the Bay of Bengal to the east, with the Indian Ocean to the south. The state capital is Chennai (formerly known as Madras), one of the largest metropolitan cities in India.
Popular tourist destinations include the hill stations of Ooty and Kodaikanal, the historical temples of Madurai and Rameswaram, and the scenic beaches of Marina and Kanyakumari.
