Telangana Telangana is a culturally rich and historically significant state in southern India, formed in 2014 as the 29th state of the country

Its capital, Hyderabad, is a bustling metropolis known for iconic landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the grand palaces of the Nizams, alongside its modern IT hubs and startups.

Telangana continues to grow as a center for education, technology, and tourism, while preserving its deep-rooted heritage.
