Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state in the southeastern part of India, known for its rich cultural heritage, historic temples, and beautiful coastline along the Bay of Bengal.

It is home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Tirupati, the scenic Araku Valley, and the ancient Buddhist site of Amaravati.
The state is also known for its vibrant festivals, spicy cuisine, and the bustling port city of Visakhapatnam (Vizag).
Andhra Pradesh also boasts stunning landscapes, including the lush Eastern Ghats and the serene backwaters of Konaseema.
