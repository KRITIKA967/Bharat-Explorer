Kerala

Kerala , located in the southwestern corner of India, is known as "God's Own Country" for its lush green landscapes, tranquil backwaters, and beautiful beaches along the Arabian Sea.

It is famous for its unique culture, classical dance forms like Kathakali, and Ayurvedic wellness traditions. Kerala is also home to stunning hill stations like Munnar, wildlife sanctuaries like Periyar, and the serene backwaters of Alleppey. The state's rich heritage, delicious cuisine, and vibrant festivals like Onam make it a top destination for travelers.
Its well-preserved ecosystems, including the Western Ghats, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, support a wide variety of flora and fauna.
